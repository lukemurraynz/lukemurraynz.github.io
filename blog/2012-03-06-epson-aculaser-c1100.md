If your **Epson AcuLaser C1100** is printing noticeably **darker on the left side** of the page, the most likely cause is a dirty or misaligned print head. This can happen over time as toner dust accumulates inside the printer.

## Clean the print head

1. Locate the **blue lever** on the **right side** of the printer.
2. **Move the lever in and out completely** several times. This actuates the internal cleaning mechanism and helps redistribute toner evenly.
3. Print a test page to check if the issue is resolved.

If the uneven printing persists after cleaning, the toner cartridge may be running low or defective and should be replaced.

## Change a toner cartridge before its expiration

You can proactively replace a toner cartridge before it runs out completely to avoid print quality degradation:

1. Make sure that **Ready** or **Sleep** is displayed on the LCD panel.
2. On the control panel, navigate to the **Reset Menu** and select **Change Toner X** (where X is **C** for Cyan, **M** for Magenta, **Y** for Yellow, or **K** for Black).
3. The printer will move the selected cartridge to the **replacement position**. Wait until the LCD displays **Replace Toner X**.
4. Open the printer cover, **remove the old cartridge**, and **insert the new one**.
5. Close the cover. The printer will automatically recognise the new cartridge.

## Maintenance tips

- **Print regularly** — infrequent use can cause toner to settle unevenly, leading to streaks and uneven density.
- **Store toner cartridges** in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
- **Shake new cartridges gently** from side to side before installing to distribute toner evenly inside the cartridge.
