Today, we are going to delve into a Secure Isolated Recovery Environment (SIRE) for Microsoft Azure pattern.

The idea of a SIRE is a pattern designed to maintain business continuity during and after cybersecurity incidents, particularly ransomware attacks.

In this blog post, we will explore the key components of a SIRE, its benefits, and how to implement it effectively within your Azure environment.
