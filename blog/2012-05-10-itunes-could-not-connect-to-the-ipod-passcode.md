If you have forgotten the passcode on your iPod and iTunes displays the error **"iTunes could not connect to the iPod because it is locked with a passcode"**, the only way to regain access is to perform a full restore through **Recovery Mode**.

> **Warning:** This process will erase all data on your iPod. If you have a previous backup in iTunes or iCloud, you can restore your content after the reset.

## How to restore using Recovery Mode

1. **Turn the iPod off** completely.
2. **Open iTunes** on your computer.
3. **Connect the iPod** to your computer using the USB cable.
4. **Press and hold the Home button** on the iPod as you connect it. Keep holding the Home button — this forces the iPod to power on and boot directly into **Recovery Mode**.
5. iTunes will display a dialog: **"iTunes has discovered an iPod in Recovery Mode. Would you like to restore?"**
6. Click **Yes** (or **Restore**).
7. Wait for the restore process to finish. The iPod will restart and load a fresh copy of iOS.

## After the restore

Once the restore completes, the iPod will display the initial setup screen. You can either set it up as a new device or **restore from a backup** if you previously synced with iTunes or iCloud.

If you continue to have issues connecting the iPod after the restore, try using a different USB cable or USB port, and make sure you are running the latest version of iTunes.
