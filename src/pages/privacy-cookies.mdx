# Privacy & Cookies

This page explains how `luke.geek.nz` uses privacy-related storage and analytics.

## Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand overall traffic patterns such as:

- page visits
- popular posts and pages
- approximate location and device information
- referral sources

Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymization enabled.

## Consent

When you first visit the site, you are shown a cookie consent banner with `Accept` and `Decline` options.

- If you accept, analytics storage is enabled for Google Analytics.
- If you decline, analytics storage remains denied.
- If you do nothing, Google Consent Mode may still send limited cookieless measurement signals to Google Analytics before consent is granted.

## What Is Stored

This site stores your banner choice in your browser under the `cookie-consent` key so the prompt does not keep appearing on every page view.
